# Pre-Project Plan: Ego Trajectory Maneuver Discovery

## Phase 1 — Data Understanding & Visualization
- Extract ego trajectories from Waymo 20s scenarios
- Recenter and align trajectories
- Visualize trajectory geometry across scenarios
- Identify dominant motion patterns qualitatively

## Phase 2 — Representation Design
- Compare position-only vs kinematic feature representations
- Explore trajectory length and time normalization
- Study sensitivity to noise and minor deviations

## Phase 3 — Unsupervised Clustering (Exploratory)
- Apply simple clustering methods (e.g., k-means, hierarchical)
- Analyze cluster stability and interpretability
- Avoid overfitting or label-driven evaluation

## Phase 4 — Interpretation & Abstraction
- Relate clusters to intuitive maneuver concepts
- Identify limitations and ambiguities
- Document insights rather than optimize metrics

## Expected Outputs
- Visual figures of normalized ego trajectories
- Cluster visualizations and summaries
- Written analysis suitable for a research report or paper draft
